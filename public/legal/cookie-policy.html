1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our Service.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
session_id Maintains your login session Session
csrf_token Security protection against cross-site attacks Session
auth_token Authentication and account access 30 days

2.2 Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics - distinguishes users 2 years
_ga_* Google Analytics - session tracking 2 years
_gid Google Analytics - distinguishes users 24 hours
mixpanel_* User behavior analytics 1 year

2.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
user_preferences Stores your settings and preferences 1 year
language Remembers your language preference 1 year
theme Remembers your display preferences 1 year

2.4 Marketing and Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign effectiveness.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_fbp Facebook Pixel - tracks conversions 3 months
_gcl_au Google Ads - conversion tracking 3 months
linkedin_* LinkedIn advertising and analytics 2 years

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics and performance tracking
  • Google Ads: Advertising and conversion tracking
  • Facebook Pixel: Social media advertising and analytics
  • LinkedIn Insight Tag: Professional network advertising
  • Mixpanel: User behavior and product analytics
  • Hotjar: User experience and heatmap analysis
  • Intercom: Customer support and messaging

4. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: Keep you logged in and secure
  • Preferences: Remember your settings and choices
  • Analytics: Understand how you use our Service
  • Performance: Optimize website speed and functionality
  • Marketing: Show relevant advertisements
  • Security: Protect against fraud and abuse
  • Personalization: Customize your experience

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

5.1 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

5.2 Opt-Out Links

You can opt out of specific tracking services:

  • Google Analytics: Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Google Ads: Google Ad Settings
  • Facebook: Facebook Ad Preferences
  • LinkedIn: LinkedIn Ad Preferences

5.3 Cookie Consent Management

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner where you can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your preferences by category
  • Learn more about each cookie type

6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies, some features may not work properly:

  • You may need to log in repeatedly
  • Your preferences won't be saved
  • Some personalization features may be unavailable
  • We won't be able to remember your consent choices
  • Analytics and improvement efforts may be limited

7. Mobile App Tracking

Our mobile applications may use similar tracking technologies:

  • Device Identifiers: IDFA (iOS), GAID (Android)
  • App Analytics: Firebase, Mixpanel
  • Crash Reporting: Crashlytics, Bugsnag
  • Push Notifications: Firebase Cloud Messaging

8. Data Retention

Cookie data is retained for different periods:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Stored for the specified duration
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized after 26 months
  • Marketing Data: Deleted after 13 months of inactivity

9. International Transfers

Cookie data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your jurisdiction. We ensure adequate protection through appropriate safeguards.
